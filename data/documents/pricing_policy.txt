SECTION 2: INTERNAL COST ESTIMATES (Monthly, USD)
================================================================

Internal cost = what the company pays the employee (salary + overhead).
Gross margin = (billing price - internal cost) / billing price × 100

Cost rate by seniority level:
  Junior:  55% of standard billing rate
  Mid:     58% of standard billing rate
  Senior:  60% of standard billing rate

Example:
  Senior Backend Developer billed at $5,500/month
  Internal cost = $5,500 × 60% = $3,300/month
  Gross margin  = ($5,500 - $3,300) / $5,500 × 100 = 40%

SECTION 3: DISCOUNT RULES
================================================================

3.1 Duration Discount
Applied based on total engagement length committed by the client.

  Under 3 months:     0% discount (not eligible — see Section 6)
  3 to 5 months:      5% discount
  6 to 11 months:    10% discount
  12 months or more: 15% discount

3.2 Team Size Discount
Applied based on number of people in the engagement.

  1-2 people:   0% discount
  3-5 people:   3% discount
  6-9 people:   5% discount
  10+ people:   8% discount

3.3 Discount Stacking Rules
Both discounts are applied MULTIPLICATIVELY, not additively.
Formula: final_price = base_price × (1 - duration_discount) × (1 - team_size_discount)

IMPORTANT: Combined effective discount is CAPPED at 25% maximum.
If calculated combined discount exceeds 25%, apply exactly 25%.

Example:
  Duration discount = 10%, Team size discount = 5%
  Combined = 1 - (1 - 0.10) × (1 - 0.05) = 1 - 0.855 = 14.5%  ← within cap

  Duration discount = 15%, Team size discount = 8%
  Combined = 1 - (1 - 0.15) × (1 - 0.08) = 1 - 0.782 = 21.8%  ← within cap

SECTION 4: SPECIAL PRICING RULES
================================================================

4.1 Part-Time Allocation
If the client requests a resource at less than full-time (e.g., 3 days/week):
  - Part-time rate = 60% of standard monthly rate
  - Applies only to DevOps, QA, and BA roles typically
  - Must be agreed upfront in the contract

4.2 Ramp-Up Discount
For the first 2 weeks when a substitute or close-match resource needs to learn the domain:
  - Ramp-up rate = 50% of standard rate for that 2-week period
  - Only applicable when the match type is "substitute" or "close"
  - Helps offset risk for the client during onboarding

4.3 Urgency Premium
If the client requires the team to start within 5 business days:
  - Urgency premium = +5% added to the final price AFTER all discounts
  - This covers priority scheduling and potential overtime during handover

SECTION 5: POLICY CONSTRAINTS & VALIDATION
================================================================

5.1 Minimum Gross Margin
All engagements must meet the minimum margin policy BEFORE approval:

  Standard account:   minimum 30% gross margin
  Strategic account:  minimum 20% gross margin
    (Strategic = existing client with >$200k historical revenue OR key reference client)

If proposed pricing produces margin below the minimum, the quote MUST be revised.
Acceptable adjustments: increase billing price, reduce team size, skip tentative roles.

5.2 Minimum Engagement Duration
  Standard minimum: 3 months
  Exception allowed for: 3 or more people engagements (may go down to 2 months)
  No exceptions for single-person or 2-person engagements below 3 months.

5.3 Seniority Substitution Rules
If the exact seniority is unavailable, one level downward substitution is allowed:
  - Senior requested → Mid provided: allowed (adjust rate to Mid rate, flag to client)
  - Mid requested → Junior provided: allowed only with written client approval
  - More than 1 level down (Senior → Junior): NOT allowed without Head of Sales sign-off

5.4 Team Composition Recommendations
These are not hard rules but should be flagged to the sales team:
  - 3 or more developers: QA Engineer is strongly recommended
  - 5 or more developers: Business Analyst is strongly recommended
  - All-junior team: Senior lead is required (at least 1 senior or mid)

SECTION 6: APPROVAL MATRIX
================================================================

Quote approval requirements based on discount level and margin:

No approval needed:
  - Combined discount ≤ 10% AND margin ≥ 35%

Sales Manager approval:
  - Combined discount 11-20% OR margin 28-34%
  - Any part-time allocation

Head of Sales approval:
  - Combined discount 21-25% OR margin 20-27%
  - Strategic account pricing (20% minimum margin applied)
  - Any seniority downgrade more than 1 level

CEO approval:
  - Any discount above 25% cap (should not happen — escalate as exception)
  - Margin below 20% (even for strategic accounts)
  - Engagement below minimum duration without team-size exception

SECTION 7: WORKED EXAMPLE
================================================================

Client: FinTech Startup ABC
Team requested: 1 Senior Backend Dev, 1 Mid Frontend Dev, 1 QA Engineer (part-time)
Duration: 6 months

Step 1 — Base rates:
  Senior Backend Dev:  $5,500 × 6 months = $33,000
  Mid Frontend Dev:    $3,500 × 6 months = $21,000
  QA Engineer (PT):    $2,800 × 60% × 6 months = $10,080
  Total before discounts: $64,080

Step 2 — Duration discount (6 months → 10%):
  $64,080 × 0.90 = $57,672

Step 3 — Team size discount (3 people → 3%):
  $57,672 × 0.97 = $55,942

Step 4 — Check cap: combined = 1 - (0.90 × 0.97) = 12.7% ← under 25% cap

Step 5 — Internal cost:
  Senior Backend: $3,300 × 6 = $19,800
  Mid Frontend: $2,030 × 6 = $12,180   (58% of $3,500)
  QA (PT): $1,540 × 60% × 6 = $5,544   (55% of $2,800 × 60%)
  Total internal cost: $37,524

Step 6 — Gross margin:
  ($55,942 - $37,524) / $55,942 = 32.9% ← passes 30% minimum

Step 7 — Approval: 12.7% discount, 32.9% margin → Sales Manager approval needed

Final quote: $55,942 for 6 months. Approved with Sales Manager sign-off.
